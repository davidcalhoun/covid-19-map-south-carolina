As South Carolina moves from containment
strategy to a disease mitigation strategy,
there's less focus on the number of
individual cases in an area and more focus
on limiting overall spread throughout the
state. It's important to know:
•

•

There are many people within our
communities who have the virus and
have never been tested, and because of
this, we’ll never have information that
accurately represents every case of
COVID-19.
Regardless of the number of reported
cases within an area, all South
Carolinians should take seriously the
recommended precautions for protecting
against this disease. This geographic
listing by ZIP code is a reference to
reported positive cases, but it doesn’t
account for likely cases of COVID-19
within a community.
